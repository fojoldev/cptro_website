Frequently Asked Questions

What is SHASU FINANCE?

SHASU FINANCE is a Blockchain Security firm that focuses on the safety and security of investors. Our suite of dApps is powered by the RISE token, which can be traded on several chains.

How can I get SHASU FINANCE?

SHUSU FINANCE is available on the DeFi Exchanges Pancake Swap (Binance Smart Chain), Uniswap (Ethereum), and QuickSwap (QuickSwap) (Polygon).

What exactly is a "Buyback"?

During times of uncertainty, SHUSU FINANCE's contract wallet (nicknamed The Kraken) can be used to add more stability to the price of SHUSU FINANCE. This is done in traditional markets as equity, and SHUSU FINANCE was the first to bring it to the crypto market. RISE investors can be more confident that their investment will hold its value even if the market falls.
